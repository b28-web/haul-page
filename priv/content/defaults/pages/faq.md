# Frequently Asked Questions

## What do you haul?

Pretty much anything that isn't hazardous. Furniture, appliances, yard waste, construction debris, electronics, mattresses — if you can point at it, we can probably haul it.

## How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on volume. We charge by the truckload:

| Load Size | Price Range |
|-----------|-------------|
| Minimum (single item) | $75 - $125 |
| Quarter truck | $150 - $250 |
| Half truck | $250 - $400 |
| Full truck | $400 - $600 |

We always quote before we load — no surprises.

## Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! Call or book before noon and we'll do our best to get there the same day. Next-day appointments are almost always available.

## What happens to my stuff?

We sort everything we haul. Usable items go to local donation centers. Recyclables go to recycling facilities. The rest is disposed of responsibly at licensed facilities.

## Do I need to be home?

Not necessarily. If the items are accessible (driveway, curb, open garage), we can handle it without you being there. Just let us know when you book.
