## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does junk removal cost?

It depends on the volume. A single item like a couch or fridge starts at $75. A full truckload runs $350–$500. We give you an exact price on-site before we start — no surprises.

### Do you offer same-day service?

Yes. Call before noon and we'll be there the same day, most of the time within a couple hours. Weekend and evening appointments available too.

### What can't you take?

Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, asbestos), medical waste, and full-size cars. Pretty much everything else, we can handle.

### Do you donate items?

Absolutely. We partner with local charities in the Bay Area. Anything in usable condition gets donated instead of trashed. We'll give you a donation receipt if you want one.

### How do I book?

Use the booking form on this site, call us at (415) 555-0147, or text us. We'll confirm a time window and show up ready to work.

### Do you do estate cleanouts?

Yes — it's one of our specialties. We handle full-property cleanouts for estates, rental turnovers, and foreclosures. We sort, haul, donate, and leave the place broom-clean.

### Are you insured?

Yes. Fully insured and licensed. We carry general liability and workers' comp.
