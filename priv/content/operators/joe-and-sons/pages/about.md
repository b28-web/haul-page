## Three Generations of Getting It Done

Joe Marchetti started hauling junk out of a borrowed pickup in 2009. Fifteen years later, Joe & Sons is a three-truck operation serving the entire Bay Area — from the Sunset to San Jose, Marin to the East Bay.

We're a family business. Joe's sons Marco and Danny run crews full-time. Every job gets the same treatment: show up on time, give an honest price, haul everything, and leave the place cleaner than we found it.

### What we believe

- **Honest pricing.** We quote what we charge. No hidden fees, no "oh, that's extra."
- **Same-day service.** Call before noon, we're there today. Guaranteed.
- **Eco-friendly disposal.** We donate what we can, recycle what's recyclable, and landfill only as a last resort.
- **Respect your space.** We wear booties inside, lay down tarps, and never scratch your floors.

### Coverage area

We serve San Francisco, Daly City, South San Francisco, San Mateo, Redwood City, Palo Alto, Mountain View, San Jose, Oakland, Berkeley, and everywhere in between.

Got a job outside our area? Call us anyway — we'll figure it out.
