# Frequently Asked Questions

## How much does junk removal cost?

Pricing depends on how much space your items take in our truck. We give you an upfront quote on-site before we start loading — no obligation. Most single-item pickups start at $75.

## Do you offer same-day service?

Yes. If you book before noon, we can usually get to you the same day. Next-day appointments are always available.

## What items do you take?

Almost everything — furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, hot tubs, and general household junk. We can't take hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, asbestos) or vehicles.

## Do you donate items?

Absolutely. Usable furniture, clothing, and household goods go to local donation centers. We recycle metals, electronics, and cardboard. Landfill is our last resort.

## What areas do you serve?

We cover Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, and most of Travis and Williamson counties.

## How do I book?

Use the booking form on this site, or call us at (512) 555-0198. We'll confirm your appointment within the hour.
