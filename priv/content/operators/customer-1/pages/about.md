# About Rapid Haul

Rapid Haul started in 2022 with one truck and a simple idea: junk removal shouldn't cost a fortune or take all week. We serve Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, and the surrounding area with same-day and next-day pickup.

## Why Customers Choose Us

- **Same-day service** — Book before noon and we're there today.
- **Upfront pricing** — We quote on-site before loading. No surprises.
- **Eco-friendly** — We donate usable items and recycle what we can. Landfill is the last resort.
- **Fully insured** — Your property is protected.

## How It Works

1. **Book online or call** — Tell us what needs to go.
2. **We quote on-site** — Our crew gives you a price before we touch anything.
3. **We haul it away** — You approve, we load, and your space is clear.

No contracts, no hidden fees, no junk left behind.
