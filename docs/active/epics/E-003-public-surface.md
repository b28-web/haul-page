## Public Surface Quality

The customer-facing pages (`/` and `/book`) are the product. They must be fast, accessible, and look right on every device and in print.

## Ongoing concerns

- Landing page loads in under 1 second on 3G
- No layout shifts — fonts preloaded, images sized
- Print stylesheet produces a clean, low-ink flyer
- Mobile-first: touch targets ≥ 44px, readable without zoom
- Booking form validates in real-time, handles photo upload from mobile camera
- SEO basics: meta tags, structured data, semantic HTML
- Operator config (name, phone, colors) renders correctly across all pages
